Stephen's Idea
Backpack Extender
Standard student and city workers have small comfortable backpacks that are uncomfortable to wear for longer stints with heavier weights.
A backpack extender would be a frame that would clasp around the waist and clamp a standard backpack to that would allow the weight of the backpack to be carried on the waist instead of the shoulders.
